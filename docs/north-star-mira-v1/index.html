FAQ

Common questions before the first drop.

How often should I replace the refill insert?

Most homes replace every 4 to 6 weeks depending on burn rhythm and room size.

Will each vessel look exactly the same?

No. The glaze is hand-applied, so slight tone shifts and grain variations are part of each piece.

Can I reuse the vessel with seasonal scents?

Yes. The insert system is designed for swap-ins so your ceramic form remains constant through seasonal scent drops.
