Front Door Concept v1

From business signals to a full launch kit, not a one-shot webpage.

AI Web Factory is a guided launch-kit studio. It asks business, market, conversion, and design questions first, then generates a structured Launch Brief an agent can execute into site, copy, offer, campaigns, QA, and next-step assets.

Start business brief View example launch kits

Why prompt-only builders fail for many businesses

  • Prompt quality depends on strategy users do not yet have.
  • Offer and conversion logic are often undefined.
  • Outputs stop at pages, with weak launch assets.
  • Visual style choices are made before business clarity exists.

Why guided intake performs better

  • Business maturity and audience are captured first.
  • Conversion goal and trust priorities shape output scope.
  • Visual boldness and motion are controlled explicitly.
  • A Launch Brief is generated before production execution.

Business input → Launch Brief → Agent-created launch kit

The visual anchor of this concept is an operating surface that traces intake decisions to generated outputs and QA gates.

1) Business Input Layer

Industry, maturity, offer, audience, trust intent, visual risk level, motion/3D preference, package scope.

  • Industry selector
  • Conversion goals
  • Must-avoid styles

2) Launch Brief Compiler

Structured brief synthesis with completeness score, missing fields, risk notes, and generation instructions.

  • Brief completeness
  • Risk notes
  • Agent instructions

3) Launch Kit Stack

Website, copy packs, pricing/offer architecture, campaign assets, QA/critic pass, and next-step recommendations.

  • Site + copy
  • Campaign assets
  • QA gate
