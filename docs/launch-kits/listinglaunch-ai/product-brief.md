# ListingLaunch AI Product Brief

## Positioning
ListingLaunch AI is a listing launch system for real estate professionals that turns one property intake into a polished, compliant, multi-channel marketing package in under an hour.

## One-line commercial promise
Turn every new property into a polished multi-channel launch in under an hour.

## Primary conversion goal
- Primary: Book a demo.
- Secondary: Start a trial.

## Target customers
- Independent real estate agents.
- Boutique real estate teams.
- Listing coordinators.
- Brokerages that need faster listing launches across multiple agents.

## Core buyer problem
Agents are expected to launch every listing across channels quickly, but the process is fragmented, repetitive, and quality-inconsistent:
- Listing notes and feature bullets are scattered across texts, docs, and MLS exports.
- Copy quality varies by listing and by team member.
- Social, email, ad, and open-house assets are rebuilt from scratch each time.
- Sellers expect polished communication and visibility, not draft-level marketing.
- Compliance and brand consistency checks happen too late.

## Product promise and offer
ListingLaunch AI ingests:
- Property address.
- Listing notes.
- Photo/feature bullets.
- Target buyer profile.

Then generates a complete launch package:
- Listing description.
- Property landing page copy and section plan.
- Social post set.
- Email announcement.
- Open-house campaign sequence.
- Ad angle set.
- Seller update summary.
- Launch checklist.

## Why this is not a generic AI writing tool
- It is workflow-structured around a listing launch, not an empty prompt box.
- It creates coordinated channel outputs from one property intake.
- It enforces brokerage voice and compliance guardrails.
- It includes launch readiness tracking and seller-facing communication outputs.
- It is designed for real-estate launch speed, not generic content experiments.

## Business model assumptions
- SaaS subscription with team/seat scaling.
- Three plans: Starter, Pro, Brokerage.
- Demo-led motion for teams/brokerages; trial-led motion for solo agents.

## Brand personality
- Trustworthy and polished.
- Operationally fast, not frantic.
- Local-market aware.
- Practical, not hype-heavy.
- Premium, but approachable for working agents.

## Messaging pillars
1. **Speed with structure:** One intake -> all launch channels.
2. **Consistency at scale:** Brand-safe output across every listing.
3. **Seller confidence:** Clear update summaries and launch readiness.
4. **Local-market relevance:** Buyer profile and neighborhood context drive messaging.
5. **Channel readiness:** Copy and campaign outputs are publish-ready, not rough drafts.

## JTBD framing
- "When I take a new listing, help me launch everything fast so I look polished and win seller trust."
- "When my team handles multiple listings, keep every launch on-brand and on-time."
- "When I present results to sellers, give me clear launch status and campaign progress."

## Success signals for this launch kit
- Product feels category-specific to real estate listing operations.
- Offer and pricing feel believable for solo agents through brokerages.
- Five-page prototype communicates a coherent system, not a single-page concept.
- Copy reads like a commercial website a team could evaluate and buy from.
